What is Artificial Intelligence (aka: AI)?

Artificial intelligence is a branch of computer science that focuses on designing and building systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as understanding language, recognizing patterns, solving problems, making decisions, and even learning from experience, and it works by using algorithms and models that allow machines to process large amounts of data, adapt to new information, and improve their performance over time, which is why we see AI powering everyday tools like recommendation engines on streaming platforms, voice assistants on smartphones, selfâ€‘driving cars, medical diagnostic systems, and creative applications that can generate text, images, or music.
